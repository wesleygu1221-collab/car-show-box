# Car Show Box

Workspace for the OMODA & JAECOO-focused Car Show Box system, covering brand, product, strategy, European market research, case studies, pitches, prompts, assets, and decks.

## Folder Structure

- `01_brand_system/` - Brand positioning, messaging, identity rules, and brand narrative.
- `02_product_system/` - Product architecture, feature notes, offer structure, and product documentation.
- `03_omoda & jaecoo_strategy/` - OMODA & JAECOO strategy, campaign thinking, partnership ideas, and market entry notes.
- `04_europe_market/` - European market research, audience insights, competitor notes, and regional opportunities.
- `05_case_studies/` - Reference cases, proof points, outcomes, and customer or dealer examples.
- `06_pitch_system/` - Pitch structure, sales angles, objection handling, scripts, and proposal frameworks.
- `07_assets/` - Source PDFs, images, logos, screenshots, video references, and reusable media.
- `08_prompts/` - AI prompts, content generation recipes, research prompts, and workflow prompts.
- `09_decks/` - Pitch decks, strategy decks, sales decks, and presentation exports.
