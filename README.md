# Car Show Box

Organized workspace for brand materials, product information, automotive context, case studies, sales scripts, pitch decks, assets, templates, and market research.

## Folder Structure

- `01_brand/` - Brand strategy, positioning, messaging, and identity notes.
- `02_product/` - Product details, features, specs, and roadmap material.
- `03_automotive/` - Automotive industry context, references, and partner notes.
- `04_case_studies/` - Customer stories, examples, proof points, and outcomes.
- `05_sales_scripts/` - Sales talk tracks, objection handling, email scripts, and call flows.
- `06_pitch_decks/` - Investor, partner, customer, and event presentation decks.
- `07_assets/` - Images, logos, videos, screenshots, and other reusable media.
- `08_templates/` - Reusable docs, slide templates, email templates, and frameworks.
- `09_market_research/` - Market analysis, competitors, trends, reports, and raw research.
