# OMODA & JAECOO Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1 - Car Show Box for OMODA & JAECOO

核心信息：A mobile premium car showroom for brand visibility, product education and dealer lead generation.

建议视觉：Car Show Box / Viewbox 外观图 + OMODA & JAECOO 车型。

## Slide 2 - The Challenge

核心信息：New automotive brands in Europe need trust before conversion.

讲点：
- 消费者未必主动进入展厅。
- 新品牌需要被看见、被理解、被体验。
- 经销商需要更多展厅之外的获客入口。

## Slide 3 - The Opportunity

核心信息：High-footfall locations can become temporary brand touchpoints.

场景：
- Shopping malls
- City plazas
- Events
- Festivals
- Mixed-use developments

## Slide 4 - The Solution

核心信息：Car Show Box brings the showroom to the customer.

内容：
- 单车展示
- Host / sales conversation
- Lead capture
- Test-drive booking
- Dealer handover

## Slide 5 - Why It Fits OMODA & JAECOO

核心信息：OMODA = urban tech, JAECOO = urban adventure.

内容：
- OMODA 适合城市科技和设计体验。
- JAECOO 适合都市越野、AWD、混动和安全表达。

## Slide 6 - Viewbox Proof

核心信息：Premium modular spaces already used by automotive and global brands.

案例：
- Kia - IAA Mobility
- Porsche Panamera
- Porsche - TNW
- Porsche - European Open
- Coca-Cola Brussels
- Booking.com UEFA EURO 2024

## Slide 7 - Product Specs

核心信息：A proven modular platform.

参数：
- 15 m2 module
- 5.9 m x 2.5 m x 3 m
- 2.5 tonnes
- 500 kg/m2 live load
- TUV certification
- LED, windows, wall panels, doors, rails

## Slide 8 - Benelux Pilot

核心信息：Start controlled, validate before scaling.

建议：
- 1 box
- Netherlands + Belgium
- 3 to 4 locations
- 1 to 3 weeks per location
- dealer-linked follow-up

## Slide 9 - Operating Model

核心信息：Each deployment connects location, logistics, brand team and dealer.

展示：
- Brand team
- Venue
- Logistics partner
- Host
- Dealer
- CRM / lead flow

## Slide 10 - Risk Control

核心信息：Feasibility is managed through hard gates.

硬门槛：
- Legal and predictable transport
- Obtainable permits
- Insurance coverage
- Plausible commercial use

## Slide 11 - Measurement

核心信息：The pilot must be judged by data, not impressions.

KPI：
- Visitors
- Qualified conversations
- QR scans
- Leads
- Test-drive bookings
- Dealer follow-up
- Cost per lead

## Slide 12 - Recommendation

核心信息：Approve a Benelux pilot validation brief.

结尾：
The next step is not a full rollout. It is a controlled pilot that validates feasibility, brand response and dealer conversion.
